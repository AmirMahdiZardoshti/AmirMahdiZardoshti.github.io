About the Workshop

This workshop brings together researchers developing and applying general-purpose machine-learned force fields for atomistic simulations in chemistry and materials science. Participants will explore theoretical foundations, symmetry-aware architectures, training strategies, and deployment in real research workflows. The goal is to deepen understanding, encourage broader adoption, and foster a collaborative scientific community around data-driven force fields.
