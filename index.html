General-Purpose Machine-Learned Force Fields in Theory and Practice

July 13–15, 2026 • University of Luxembourg

About the Workshop

This workshop brings together researchers developing machine-learned force fields for atomistic simulations in chemistry and materials science. Participants will explore theory, training, validation, and applications in real workflows.
